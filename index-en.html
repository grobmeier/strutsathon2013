Strutsathon 2013

We are hacking Apache Struts in Augsburg (Germany).

We meet on friday at the Java Usergroup Augsburg for presentations, and on Saturday we work on the Apache Struts 2 core.

Training

Friday, 06.09.2013

Four Struts-Comitter and members of the Apache Software Foundation explain in almost three hours the interals of Struts 2. All speakers have a long and in-depth experience with the framework and are actively developing it at Apache.

There will be two breaks and two Live-Coding sessions between the talks.

Would you like to learn about Struts 2?

Hackathon

Saturday, 07.09.2013

We are hacking on saturday!

If you would like to learn even more on Struts 2 the Hackthon is your chance! Seats are limited, register quickly.

Struts 2 beginners are welcome to join the hackathon.

Maybe Struts 2 will become Struts 3 soon. Everybody on the Hackathon can make it happen. Four Struts core developers will answer your questions and help you to make your way. What we build on this day, will most likely be included to Struts 3.

Would you like to learn more?
