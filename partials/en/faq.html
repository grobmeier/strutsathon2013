Frequently asked questions

Do I need to attend on both days?

No, just attend the day you like most. Both days are independent to each other. But if you don't have a clue on Struts but would like to attend the Hackathon, it is recommended to join the training day as well.

Do I become an Apache-committer when I participate the Hackathon?

Unfortunately not :-) You can become a committer when you demonstrate a long term interest on Struts. At the hackathon we'll work like we do at Apache and you'll learn a lot about the project. After that it is up to you if you would like to stick with the project or not.

Why do I need an ICLA?

We would like to use your code in Struts and need your permission. The ICLA gives us that permission. Although it is not required to submit an ICLA to join the hackathon, it would be great. It's simply difficult to separate your code from the code of others.

I have questions. Whom can I ask?

Christian is organizing the event and answers your questions. Please look for his e-mail address in the Imprint.
