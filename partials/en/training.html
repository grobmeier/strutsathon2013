Training

This evening will take its time. In almost 3 hours you will learn about the magic of Apache Struts 2. Struts 2 is a modern framework and this will be demostrated.

After a Struts 2 teaser we are going into depth. We discuss "interceptors", "OGNL" and the "ValueStack". We will not miss out web application security.

There will be two live coding demos. The "Web Frameworks Radar" will show you how Struts relates to other frameworks.

After this evening you know how to use Struts 2 - and when.

All information comes from Apache Struts committers and Project Management Comittee members. Most of them have long years experience and know each any aspect of the framework. Questions welcome.

An evening like that has usually a high cost, but this one is free. Instead we have different motivations: we would like to prepare you for the Strutsathon, a whole day hackathon around Struts.

Note: please let us know if you are coming to the training day and if you prefer english as presentation language.

Content

  • Struts Teaser (Rene)
  • Live Demo: Jumpstart Struts 2 (Rene)
  • Struts in Detail (Team)
Break after 60 Minuten.
  • Web-Frameworks Radar: Comparision to other frameworks (Rene)
  • Live Demo: Feature Showcase: Coding with jQuery/Bootstrap (Johannes)
Break after 40 Minuten.
  • Struts Security Thoughts (Lukasz)
  • Developing Open Source at the Apache Software Foundation (Rene, Christian)
End after 40 Minuten.

Speaker

Rene Gielen is Chair of the Apache Struts project and works as an independent consultant and trainer. He knows Struts 2 since the early beginning and is active even after 7 years of committing to Struts.

Johannes Geppert works as developer and architect for an international logistics and e-commerce company. Besides his work on the Struts core, he maintains jQuery/Bootstrap UI components for Struts 2.

Lukasz Lenart is developer at SoftwareMill and one of the leading committers at Struts. He has an in-depth knowledge of Struts and the one who is patching the most security holes.

Christian Grobmeier is independent developer, trainer and Chair of the Apache Logging project. He is active at Apache Struts for two years.
